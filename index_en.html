DEVELOPMENT OF THE SYSTEM OF DEMONSTRATION
OF ACHIEVEMENTS OF THE PHARMACEUTICAL
AND MEDICAL INDUSTRIES OF THE RUSSIAN FEDERATION 2013
Within the framework of the Federal Target Program “Development of the Pharmaceutical and Medical Industry of the Russian Federation For the Period Up to 2020 and Beyond”, the project to develop a system to demonstrate the achievements and support the market launch of the products of the domestic pharmaceutical and medical industries is being implemented.

The project is implemented in three main areas:

  • The organization and demonstration of the collective expositions and presentation of the “Innovations for the patient” key multimedia project, representing the developments of domestic producers, supported under the federal target program, at the main professional Russian and foreign venues.
  • Organizing and conducting independent event – Road show – at the sites of the regional pharmaceutical clusters. The event includes a demonstration of collective exposure and a round table on the current topic.
  • Organizing participation of various speakers (business, government and science representatives) in the thematic round tables, forums and congresses.

Round table “HOW TO STRENGHTEN THE ROLE OF THE RUSSIAN PHARMACEUTICAL INDUSTRY IN THE GLOBAL MARKET”

Date:
June 20, 2013
Venue:
Hall 7, meeting room 7.17
Time:
14:00 - 15:30

The general concept of the event:

The purpose of the event is to provide the main market players with an opportunity to share their views on the prospects for the pharmaceutical and medical industry of the Russian Federation, to improve its competitiveness in the global market. The experts will give their assessment of the government measures aimed at creating a favorable climate for the development of new medicines in the Russian Federation, they will discuss the real success stories of domestic manufacturers of drugs and medical equipment.

This discussion will initiate a series of events sponsored by the Ministry of Industry and Trade of the Russian Federation aimed at strengthening cooperation between the major players in the industry, and promoting and enhancing the competitiveness of the Russian pharmaceutical sector in the global market. The dialogue between the leading international experts, representatives of public authorities and the business community will be continued at the Moscow International Forum for Innovative Development “Open Innovations”, which will be held in Moscow from October 31 to November 2, 2013. In the course of this discussion the most significant breakthrough technologies, which can significantly influence the development of the pharmaceutical market in the near future, will be highlighted. Also directions of the effective long-term cooperation between the Russian and international businesses in the field of biotechnology and medicine will be discussed and highlighted.


Government support tools of the biopharmaceutical industry in the Russian Federation.

What conditions should be established by the government in order to improve the competitiveness of the Russian pharmaceutical industry? / Federal Target Program “Development of the Pharmaceutical and Medical Industry of the Russian Federation for the Period Up To 2020 and Beyond” as an instrument of state policy in the pharmaceutical industry.

The role of development institutions in enhancing the innovation capacity of pharmaceutical industry.

Big Pharma in Russia. Technology transfer and localization in the Russian market.

How to evaluate the importance/competitiveness of the Russian pharmaceutical industry on a global level? Where is the economic balance between the competition for the role in the global market and solution of the intra-Russian problems of the industry specified in the “Pharma 2020” program?

The commercial potential of the products of the modern domestic medical industry.

New trends in the global pharmaceutical industry.

Partnership between the Russian Federation and the United States of America as a unique opportunity to introduce innovative products to the Russian market.
